# Voice Brief: AI Equity Framework

## 1. Single-sentence thesis
The framework closes the launch window gap by calibrating fairness alarm thresholds via pre-launch synthetic simulation, and enforcing those exact thresholds via post-launch production monitoring.

## 2. Genre
Methodological. A structured walkthrough of a system architecture.

## 3. Speaker
Dr. Heather Leffew. Third-person default. First-person `I` used only when she is the genuine agent of a design choice (e.g., choosing RandomForest over deep models for interpretability). The grammatical subject is the system, the architecture, or the monitor.

## 4. Negative-space declaration
This piece does not discuss regulatory compliance frameworks (like OFCCP) as its primary subject; it does not perform "compassion" or use ethical-panic language (no "chillingly cold" or "terrifying"). It avoids all SaaS marketing register (no "leverages", "empowers", "unlocks", "seamless").

## 5. Section spine
1. **Front Matter**: Abstract, metadata, byline grid.
2. **01 / The Launch Window Gap**: Defining the problem between intuition-driven review and data-driven post-launch audits.
3. **02 / Mode 1: Pre-Launch Simulation**: The calibration engine, synthetic generation, and the RandomForest bias injection.
4. **03 / Setting the Threshold (Interactive Simulator)**: The interactive visualizer showing how effect size and cohort size determine the alarm threshold.
5. **04 / The Intersectional Schema**: Breaking down the 40+ variables, specifically the inferred accessibility score.
6. **05 / Mode 2: Post-Launch Monitoring**: The scheduled drift analysis and webhook alerting.
7. **06 / Limits and what this is not**: What the framework commits to and what it does not.

## 6. Opening sentence
The problem the framework solves is the gap between intuition-driven fairness review and data-driven fairness review.

## 7. Closing sentence
What the framework offers is a structural guarantee: an alarm threshold calibrated against historical reality before launch, and enforced mechanically after launch.

- Refuse to use performing-arts or textile analogies (no "orchestrating the data" or "weaving the signals").
